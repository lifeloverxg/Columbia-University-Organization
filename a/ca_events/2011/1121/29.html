CU-Asia: Career Development Presentation For International Students on Nov. 30th

We invite you to a Career Development Presentation by the Center for Career Education (CCE) for Columbia University Asia-Pacific Development Society (CU-Asia) which will address specific challenges that many Asian international students face in obtaining employment opportunity in the US and how one can manage these challenges by implementing effective job strategies and utilizing career resources through CCE. The second part of the presentation will be delivered by an immigration attorney who will address legal considerations for international students including the OPT/H1-B/and Permanent Residency in the US. Please arrive early for the event. Sign-in will begin promptly at 2:45pm. Click here to pre-register.

 
Career Development Presentation for the Columbia University Asia-Pacific Development Society (CU-Asia)
Date: Wednesday, November 30th, 2011
Time: 3 – 4:30pm
Location: Center for Career Education Conference Room 
-------------------------------------------------------
 

PART ONE (3:00 – 3:45pm)
I. Challenges & Strategies for International Students in Job Search in the US
II.  Career Resources for International Students
III. US Labor Statistics & H-1B Visa Information
  - US Market Trend: Job Openings Rate Labor Turnover from 09/08- 09/11
  - Job Outlook in Finance & Consulting/Science & Technology/ Social and Human Services 
  - Top 100 H1B Visa Sponsors in the US - 2011 H1B Visa Report 
  - H-1B Visa Employers for New York
 
PART TWO (3:45 – 4:30pm)
Immigration Attorney Presentation on the OPT/H1-B/Permanent Residency

 

Should you have any questions, please send it to cuasia.event@gmail.com
