Time: 6:30pm - 8:30pm  Tuesday. May 12nd, 2009

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

ENN Group, focusing on clean energy industry, takes on energy development and utilization method innovation as its basis, and regards technology innovation as its traction. Based on energy value chain, the Group has formed an industrial chain integrating energy exploration, conversion, distribution and logistics, which is developed collaboratively with industries including energy equipment, intelligence technology, industrial estate, biochemical, etc. Moreover, ENN has established platform for technology promotion, business development and talent cultivation support each other.

 

At present, ENN had set up more than 100 subsidiaries, holding companies and offices in over 80 cities across China and in countries including U.K., USA, Australia, etc., and had employed more than 23,000 people with total assets exceeding USD 4 billion.

 

The major scientific research items of ENN Science & Technology are in the field of algae bioenergy technology, catalytic coal-based gasification technology, hydrogen production technology and system energy efficiency technology.

 

 

新奥集团成立于1989年，专注于清洁能源产业，以创新能源开发和利用方式为基础，特别是以技术创新为牵引，逐步构建了涵盖能源开发、能源转化、能源分销和能源物流的产业价值链条，同时也投资于能源装备、智能科技、园区地产和生物化工等产业，形成协同发展的产业群，是中国重要的能源开发利用企业之一。

 

新奥科技发展有限公司隶属于新奥集团，致力于为新奥能源战略的持续发展提供技术支撑，主要科研项目分布在光伏能源、生物质能源、煤基清洁能源生产、清洁能源应用和节能减排等5个领域，已初步组建了一支国际化的科技研发和技术管理团队。随着集团战略的迅速推进，吸引海内外高科技人才、尽快形成国际水平的研发能力已成为集团的当务之急，为此，我集团面向海外招聘贤才，重点是美国这样的科技发展水平高、中国留学生聚集的发达国家.

 

 

Thank you for your interest!
