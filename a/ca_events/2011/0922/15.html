Dear Chinese Students and Scholars,

 

You have made through a challenging journey to pursue top tier education here in the US. While you should be proud of your supreme education background, you may wonder how to utilize this advantage to begin or advance your career. At IBM, we unite different cultures, languages, professions and perspectives in one globally integrated enterprise. This approach is the cornerstone of our business strategy and it’s helping to fuel our rapid growth in markets all over the world. As a Chinese student with education background from a top US university, you’ll have an ideal opportunity to make immediate business contributions and realize early career advancement during this explosive growth period through IBM China’s vast array of job opportunities.

 

IBM in China – A Leading Example of Commitment, Growth, Partnership and Localization

Multiple No.1 in the Market

We are No.1 in the server market

We are No.1 in the external storage market

We are No.1 in the middleware market

We are No.1 in the services market

 

 

What are your options when you START@IBM in China?

 

Research & Development Positions

IBM China Research Lab (CRL)

Business Unit Introduction: http://www-900.ibm.com/cn/crl/

 

Position: Lab Researcher (https://jobs3.netmedia1.com/cp/job_summary.jsp?job_id=RES-0204499)

 

We are looking for candidates with a postgraduate degree in Computer Science or related disciplines to conduct research in one or more of the following fields:

- Network & Telecommunication Business Intelligence

- Next Generation Web, 3D Internet & Virtual World

- Information Visualization & Interactive Visual Analytics

- Supply Chain Management & Logistics

- Retail Industry Solution and Services

- Compiler Research

 

We are seeking top-tier innovative researchers with "mind and hand", who are passionate in both technology innovation and applying the technology in real world. The candidates should have an MS or PhD degree in computer science or related fields. You should have good research and development skills, the ability to learn new areas quickly, excellent teaming and communication skills.

 

IBM China Development Lab (CDL)

Business Unit Introduction: http://www-900.ibm.com/cn/cdl/

 

We are looking for candidates with a strong background in Computer Science or related fields to fill following positions:

- J2EE/Java Software Developer

(https://jobs3.netmedia1.com/cp/job_summary.jsp?job_id=SWG-0204488)

- C/C++ Developer

(https://jobs3.netmedia1.com/cp/job_summary.jsp?job_id=SWG-0204490)

- Software Engineer

(https://jobs3.netmedia1.com/cp/job_summary.jsp?job_id=STG-0204494)

 

Our ideal candidates must have strong technical skills, a passion for technology and software, strong teaming skills and show leadership potential. You must be able to work in a high energy environment and exhibit creative thinking and collaboration. You also should have the ability to turn ideas into reality, communicate effectively, and work closely with others in teams around the world is essential.

 

 

Business Consulting Positions

IBM Global Business Services

Business Unit Introduction: http://www-900.ibm.com/cn/services/bcs/

 

Join a leader. Consult with us!

 

When people turn to IBM consulting, what are they seeking?

They are trying to imagine what their future holds. They are looking for a unique capacity to conceptualize opportunities, analyze developments, and overcome grand challenges. At the most basic level, IBM’s defining value over the years has not been our assets, our size, or our offerings. It has been the way we think.

 

What will you do as an IBM consultant?

With diverse practice areas, our consulting services help clients develop strategy, integrate process, technology and information to reduce costs, and improve profit and shareholder value.

 

What will an IBM consulting role offer you?

- Exceptional training and benefits

- Interaction with clients with challenging responsibilities

- Career growth opportunities, mobility and flexibility

- Supportive work environment

- On-going learning and development opportunities

Overseas Chinese Student Internship Program

This internship program is geared toward current university students and experienced professionals who are pursuing advanced degrees overseas to provide them with hand-on experience and prepare them for corporate and community leadership in a variety of fields. This will be a great opportunity for overseas students who are interested in learning the operations and development opportunities within a leading multinational in China. For students who maintain an excellent academic record and a positive work performance with IBM, conversion to full time hires will be considered. Intern positions for overseas Chinese students will be available in the near future. Please stay tuned for this opportunity!

 

 

How to apply:

Please complete your online profile and submit your resume through our employment website at http://www.ibm.com/start. Direct link of application is attached to each position listed above. All resumes submitted to our career website are active for a period of six months. You will be contacted if identified as a strong match.
