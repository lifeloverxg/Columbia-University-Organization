Spring Career Fair - Friday, Feb. 24

时间:2012-09-12 10:17来源:未知 作者:RainnieZhou 点击: 次
Attend the Spring Career Fair on Friday, Feb. 24, 11am-4pm! Over 130 registered employers! Including: Cond Nast, IBM, Microsoft, Morgan Stanley, Teach for America, and U.S. Department of State. Register by Wednesday, Feb. 22 for a Fast Pass

 

Attend the Spring Career Fair on Friday, Feb. 24, 11am-4pm!

Over 130 registered employers! Including: Condé Nast, IBM, Microsoft, Morgan Stanley, Teach for America, and U.S. Department of State.

Register by Wednesday, Feb. 22 for a Fast Pass. Please make sure to have this and your Columbia ID with you to expedite your admission.

Prepare for the fair - Attend one of the following events that best fits you: 

  • Undergraduate Students:
    • Career Fair 101 for Undergraduates: Essential Preparation for the Spring Career Fair & All Ivy Environmental and Sustainable Development Career Fair + Walk Ins on Friday, Feb. 17 at 10:30am

·         Graduate Students:

o    Graduate Student Resume Reviews for Spring Career Fair on Monday, Feb. 20 at 2pm

  • All Students & Alumni:
    • Making the Most of the Career Fair on Tuesday, Feb. 21 at 5pm

We look forward to seeing you on Friday, Feb. 24 in business attire (please no jeans and sneakers)!
