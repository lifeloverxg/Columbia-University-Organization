NYDC: 感受美国历史 – 费城一日游 (4月22日)，参与不限哥大学生
NYDC: Spirit of America – Day Trip to Philadelphia (Apr. 22). Event open to public
 
        为了大家春季出游，更多感受美国的人文精神，既上学期三次出游活动后（见cuasia.org），现安排“费城一日游”，请抓紧机会，及早报名。行程如下：
 
        上午9点由哥大出发，第一站在费城百年老店Jim’s Steaks品尝Philly Cheese Steak，自行购买午餐。午餐之后游览费城主要景点，包括独立厅，自由钟等。另外，作为旧城改造的典范，费城拥有诸多著名的古典式和现代化的建筑。本次行程会有导游具体讲解参观，例如费城艺术馆等。最后，如时间允许，会安排参观宾夕法尼亚大学及沃顿商学院，并于宾大学生见面交流。预计下午5点离开费城，晚上7点左右返校。（具体行程有可能根据情况调整。）
 
        费用：车费35$。不包含午餐及景点门票。
 
        集合地点：哥大校门(Broadway @ 116th St) 
 
Take a day trip with us to Philadelphia, the birthplace of the United States. We will arrange lunch at Jim’s Steaks serving the Philly Cheese Steak, among others. (Note: the trip expense collected does not include lunch). In the afternoon, we will visit the Hall of Independence, the Liberty Bell, and surroundings. In addition, as a successful example of urban renewal, Philadelphia boasts an array of both classical and modern architecture. Our tour guide will discuss a few in detail, including Philadelphia Museum of Art. If time permits, we will also visit and meet with students from the University of Pennsylvania and the Wharton Business School. The bus is scheduled to leave at 9am from Columbia campus, returning around 7pm. (Itinerary subject to changes.) 
 
Trip expense: Bus - 35$. Lunch or tickets for touring spots are not included.
 
Pick-up location: Main Entrance of Columbia U (Broadway @ 116th St) 
 
Contact us at: cuasia.event@gmail.com
