(1) 林肯中心现代摇滚歌剧: 《猴：西游记》
      Monkey: Journey to the West
 
《猴：西游记》：给你一个驱妖降魔能舞会唱的前卫版孙悟空。现代摇滚歌剧跨界融合动画、特效、武术、杂技、流行等元素，全面席卷纽约林肯中心。
 
2013年7月，现代摇滚歌剧《猴：西游记》将作为林肯中心艺术节开幕大戏亮相大卫寇克剧院。《猴：西游记》由著名华裔导演陈士争先生执导，英国摇滚音乐鬼才戴蒙阿尔伯恩(Damon Albarn)作曲，英国获奖艺术家杰米.何立特(Jamie Hewlett)担任舞台和形象设计。
 
《猴：西游记》时长100分钟，讲述了唐僧师徒西天取经的神话故事。该剧于2007年在曼彻斯特国际艺术节首演，演出引起巨大轰动，为当地观众津津乐道。随后该剧又在法国巴黎的查特莱剧院，2008美国斯波莱托艺术节及伦敦皇家剧院上演，并在巴黎查特莱剧院创造了连演16场并加演3场的历史纪录。
 
北美华人科技工商联合会有幸负责《猴：西游记》在纽约地区的中国市场推广与票务受理。林肯中心艺术节是该剧在纽约的首次及唯一一次亮相，票量有限，售完即止

Monkey: Journey to the West- Rock Your World with a Mighty Fancy Monkey King
Combining Elements from East and West, New Rock’n’Roll Opera Makes its Debut in New York
 
In July 2013, Opening the Lincoln Center Festival is Monkey: Journey to the West, extraordinary music theater collaboration based on the classic Chinese 
 
folktale “Journey to the West.” Conceived and directed by internationally renowned opera and film director Chen Shi-Zheng, with music by Damon Albarn, lead 
 
singer of Blur, and design and animation by award-winning artist Jamie Hewlett, who, with Albarn, created the virtual band, Gorillaz.
 
The 100-minute theatrical spectacle, Monkey: Journey to the West follows the fantastical trek undertaken by a monk who travels from China to India seeking 
 
the Buddhist sacred scriptures.  On his journey through an enchanted world, led by the irrepressible Monkey King, the monk and his magical animal companions 
 
have many adventures, both comical and challenging.
 
Chinese Entrepreneurs Association In North America is in charge of tickets promotion to Chinese living in Greater New York Area. Monkey: Journey to the West 
 
will make its New York debut at Lincoln Center Festival. Tickets are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis. For more information or to

(2) 清华、南开、天大、天医校友会2013年度夏季联合野餐烧烤活动
     2013 Summer Picnic & BBQ for Alumni Associations
