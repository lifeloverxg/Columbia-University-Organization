(2) 提升公开发言能力讲座
     Improving Public Speech Skills for Your Business and Career Development

 
如何提高讲话技巧，如何与上司沟通，如何面试找工作，如何跟投资人和客户讲话才最有效是每个职场人士和创业者几乎每天都面临的事情。旅美科协大纽约分会和大纽约地区清华校友会为我们荣幸的邀请到了曼哈顿著名演讲培训大师 TJ Walker 作演讲和辅导，他多年来一直为华尔街和各大药厂、高科技企业的高管提供培训。
演讲题目： Improving Public Speech Skills for Your Business and Career Development– Presentations,  Investor/Sales Pitch Tips, Job Interview Tips, President Obama's Speech Preview, and more
演讲时间： 2013年9月28号（星期六），1:30 PM – 5:00 PM
演讲地点： 纽约大学商学院 (NYU Stern School of Business, KMC Room I-70)
演讲地址： 44 W 4th St New York, NY 10012
演讲费用： 免费，但必须注册，注册满130人截止。
网上注册： http://goo.gl/qCE6UC
详细信息请登录网站http://www.tsinghua.org/ and  http://www.castusa-gny.org/。本次活动是career development series 和 business skills series系列活动的开始，欢迎志愿者加入参与组织以后的活动。

(3) 哥伦比亚大学行政培训项目中文翻译招聘
     Experienced Mandarin Translator Needed for Columbia Executive Training Program    
 
A Columbia University Executive Education Center is looking for an experienced mandarin translator for its senior Chinese Executive Training program. The compensation will be commensurate with experience. The required dates are October 1st - 3rd, 2013 between8:30am - 4:00pm. Candidates are expected to have notable experience & knowledge of the technical language in the following topics: International Trade, Management, Economics, the U.S. and Europe Financial Crisis, Trends in Trade Investment. 
 
Interested candidates please email cuasia.event@gmail.com as soon as possible with subject [Executive Program Translator], and attach your experience and CV. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interview. Thanks
