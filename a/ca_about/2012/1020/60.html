CU-Asia 2012秋季招新

时间:2013-07-13 03:10来源:未知 作者:Frank Yao 点击: 次
哥伦比亚大学亚太发展协会（CU-Asia）现征集志愿者加入我们的行列，组成一个全新的委员会！

 

亲爱的朋友们，
    哥伦比亚大学亚太发展协会（CU-Asia）现征集志愿者加入我们的行列，组成一个全新的委员会！
 
我们是谁？
    CU-Asia（www.cuasia.org）是一个经哥伦比亚大学认可的校园组织，致力于促进美国和亚太地区（特别是大中华地区）之间的相互了解和文化交流。在过去多种多样的活动中，我们最为知名的就是举办多场精彩、高规格的讲座和演出。最近几年我们邀请或联合邀请的名人包括：前谷歌中国总裁李开复博士，百度总裁李彦宏先生，诺贝尔经济学奖得主约瑟夫*斯蒂格利茨博士，中国驻纽约总领馆总领事孙国祥大使，工商银行董事长姜建清博士，导演姜文，钢琴演奏家郎朗，表演艺术家姜昆、唐国强、张铁林等等。
 
我们在寻找谁？
    我们正在组建一个全新的委员会，因此将提供各级岗位。无论你是经验丰富与否，只要你有热情，愿意花时间与团队合作来推动社会前进，那么我们找的就是你！
 
你会得到什么？
    在一个像我们这样的非赢利组织工作不会让你荷包满满。但是你会交到新朋友，开心的工作，提升你的领导技能，扩大你的社交圈，或许也能对你的最终职业目标有利。
 
如何申请？
    请告诉我们你的基本情况以及你想在团队中担当什么样的角色，包括你的简历，甚至你想实现的活动创意（如果已经想到的话）。申请没有特定的格式。但你表述得越具体，就越可能得到你感兴趣的职位，并在新团队里发挥你的作用。
 
    请您在2012年10月24日之前报名：https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dEhJU1dCQlotdXdHWVp2RlhSV1Q2cUE6MQ#gid=0 。面试初步定为2012年10月27日，我们将通知入围候选人进一步的细节。期待着您的回音！

 
 
Dear all,
 
Columbia University Asia-Pacific Development Society (CU-Asia) is now calling for volunteers to join us and form a brand-new committee!
 
Who are we?
CU-Asia (www.cuasia.org) is a recognized Columbia campus organization devoted to promoting mutual understanding and culture exchange between U.S. and the Asia-Pacific region, particularly Greater China. Among many types of past activities, we have been best known for our high-profile distinguished lectures and performances. Notable speakers and performers we hosted or co-hosted in the recent years include Dr. Kaifu Lee, Former Google China President, Mr. Robin Li, CEO of Baidu, Dr. Joseph Stiglitz, Nobel Laureate, Mr. Sun Guoxiang, Chinese Consul-General in New York, Dr. Jiang Jianqing, Chairman of ICBC, Mr. Jiang Wen, Director, Lang Lang, Pianist, Jiang Kun, Tang Guoqiang, Zhang Tielin and many others.
 
Who are we looking for?
We are forming a brand-new committee, so positions will be available at all levels. Whether you are experienced or not, as long as you have passion and are willing to devote time working with your peer team members to carry the society forward towards its vision, you are welcome!
 
What will you get?
Volunteering at a non-profit group like us will not get you rich in monetary aspect. But you will make friends, have fun, develop your leadership skills, enlarge your social network and hopefully find it beneficial to your ultimate career goals.
 
How to apply?
Please tell us who you are and what kind of role you would like to play in the team. You may include your CV or even program ideas (if you have) which you like to pursue. There is no specific format of the application. But the more specific you can tell, the better we can match you to the roles you are interested in and good at playing in the new team.
 
Please submit your application at  https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dEhJU1dCQlotdXdHWVp2RlhSV1Q2cUE6MQ#gid=0   before Oct. 24, 2012. Interview will take place on Oct. 27, 2012. Shortlisted candidates will then be notified for further details. We look forward to hearing from you!
