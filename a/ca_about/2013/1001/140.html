CU-Asia Advisor:

Ann K. Madigan - Ann is the Student Affairs Manager in the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences at Columbia University.

 

CU-Asia Board

Charles Shen, Ph.D. - Charles is Co-Director of Advanced ConsTruction and InfOrmation techNology (ACTION) Laboratory,  and Associate Research Scientist of Civil Engineering and Engineering Mechanics at Columbia University. He is the founding president of CU-Asia, and currently serves as its Chairman of the Board.

Jianzhou Zhang, Ph.D. Candidate in Measurement, Evaluation, and Statistics - Jianzhou is a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Columbia University and was the President of Columbia University Asia Pacific Development Society(CU-Asia) in the 2013-2014 year. He joined CU-Asia board in 2013. 

Jie Zhou, Ph.D. Candidate in Biological Sciences – Jie is a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Biological Sciences Program at Columbia University. She is the President of Columbia University Asia Pacific Development Society(CU-Asia) in the 2014-2015 year. She joined CU-Asia Board in 2014.

Wei Zhao,Ph.D.

Fang Chu, Ph.D. - Fang is currently working with a New York-based private equity investment firm with primary focus on real estate investment across the US. He holds a PhD from New Jersey Institute of Technology, where he serves on the Advisory Board. He is the Co-Director of Asia Financial Society Institute. He joined CU-Asia Board in 2013.



CU-Asia External Advisors

Jim Cheng, Director, C.V. Starr East Asian Library, Columbia University

Lening Liu, Professor of East Asian Languages and Cultures, Director, Confucious Institute of Chinese Language Pedagogy, Columbia University

Kaiyuan Wang, MBA. A visiting scholar at Columbia University for two years. The bearer of the Permanent Gold Status Card of Columbia University Libraries. Executive Vice Chairman of MBA Alumni Association at Peking University. He is also a founding director of Peking University Entrepreneurs Club and a founding director of Tibet Outdoors Association.

Yunfei Xiao, the executive chairman of the American Chinese Friendship Association. Guest Professor of Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) in China. Art advisor of Princeton University CSSA from 2014. General advisor of Yale University CSSA from 2008.
